About Me

I am going to University of Michigan for CS PhD in 2024 fall. I'm currently a visiting student at MIT Computational Cognitive Science Lab. I'm fortunate to be advised by Jiayuan Mao.

Previously, I was a research assistant with Professor Shao-Hua Sun at NTU Robot Learning Lab. I received my M.S. degree from National Taiwan University, where I was advised by Professor Tian-Li Yu at Taiwan Evolutionary Intelligence Laboratory. I received my B.S. degree with double majors in Electrical Engineering and Psychology from National Taiwan University in 2021.

My curiosity centers on learning, reasoning and decision-making, as well as crafting a cognitive architecture that articulates these abilities. I also interseted in knowledge representation and neuro-symbolic system with reinforcement learning.
