PA team

From 2019 to 2020, I was actively involved in the Public Address Team in NTU Student Association and voluntarily provided technical support for student activities and performances. Specifically, I designed lighting scenes and operated consoles for each performance, which requires solid technical knowledge and being proactive in communicating with organizers and crew.

In 2020, I became the officer of lighting system and managed the auditorium stage lighting system, including the power system, digital communication networks, and stage lighting instruments.

Badminton

I have played badminton since elementary school, advancing to join my department's badminton team during my university years. In 2019, I became a member of the NTU Women's Badminton Varsity Team, and in 2023, I won gold medals in both the women's singles and the mixed team event at the NTU Cup.

By analyzing tactics and shot variations in the game, I gained insights into human cognitive processes in prediction and decision-making, fueling my interest in cognitive science research.
