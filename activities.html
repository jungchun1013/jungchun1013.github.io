Life

PA team

From 2018 to 2020, I was actively involved in the Public Address Team in NTU Student Association and voluntarily provided technical support for student activities and performances. Mainly, I designed lighting scenes and operated consoles for each performance, which requires solid technical knowledge and being proactive in communicating with organizers and crew. I also accquire theoretical knowledge and technical skills for stage such as sound recording and mixing, color theory, and stage electrics.

In 2019, I became the officer of lighting system and managed the auditorium stage lighting system, including the power system, digital communication networks, and stage lighting instruments.

Selected performance (lighting design): [Opening dance] Carpe Diem (YouTube)

Badminton

I have played badminton since elementary school, advancing to join my department's badminton team during my university years. In 2019, I became a member of the NTU Women's Badminton Varsity Team.

While analyzing tactics and shot variations in the game, I learn to observe opponent's intention, predict their next move, and make deception, which inspired me to think about human behavior and decision-making in cognitive science.
